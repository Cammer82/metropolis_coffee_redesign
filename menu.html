Our Menu

Our coffee menu showcases all of our wonderful coffees in a simple, straightforward manner. Our Baristas are very knowledgeable in espresso extraction and drink preparation, and love chatting with you about coffee. Keep checking back for special menu items that pop up from time to time, but above all else, enjoy your coffee the way you like it. Metropolis is dedicated to providing a stellar coffee experience, with our food menu complimenting our high quality coffee offerings. We are proud to be partnered with Revolution Bakeshop for a diverse assortments of savory and sweets. Monthly changing flavors of scones, muffins, and gluten free coffee cake. Chocolate, almond, and butter croissants always. We have you covered. Cheers!

Our Coffee

Metropolis Coffee and Herkimer Coffee have had a very long, good-standing relationship. Since Herkimer Coffee opened in 2003, we have been fortunate to have these amazing coffees available in our shops. The coffees Herkimer buys command a premium price in the market place - a price Herkimer Coffee pays - and that in turn ensures good wages, good workers, and good working conditions at the farmsHerkimer Coffee negotiates directly with producers, visiting farms and processing stations to cultivate a beneficial relationship for both parties.
